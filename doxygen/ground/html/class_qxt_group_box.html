Detailed Description

The QxtGroupBox widget is a collapsive and checkable QGroupBox.

QxtGui QxtGroupBox is a checkable group box automatically expanding/collapsing its content according to the check state. QxtGroupBox shows its children when checked and hides its children when unchecked.

"Two QxtGroupBoxes - an expanded and a collapsed - on top of each other."
