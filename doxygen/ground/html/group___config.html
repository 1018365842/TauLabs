Compute the scale factor and bias associated with a vector observer according to the equation: B_k = (I_{3x3} + D)^{-1} (O^T A_k H_k + b + ) where k is the sample index, B_k is the kth measurement I_{3x3} is a 3x3 identity matrix D is a 3x3 symmetric matrix of scale factors O is the orthogonal alignment matrix A_k is the attitude at the kth sample b is the bias in the global reference frame is the noise at the kth sample

After computing the scale factor and bias matrices, the optimal estimate is given by {B}_k = (I_{3x3} + D)B_k - b

This implementation makes the assumption that is a constant white, gaussian noise source that is common to all k. The misalignment matrix O is not computed.
