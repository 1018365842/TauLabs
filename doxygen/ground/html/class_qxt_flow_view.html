Detailed Description

The QxtFlowView widget is an item view for images with impressive flow effects.

QxtGui A widget for showin images with animation effects, like Apple's Cover Flow (in iTunes and iPod). Images are arranged in form of slides, one main slide is shown at the center with few slides on the left and right sides of the center slide. When the next or previous slide is brought to the front, the whole slides flow to the left or to the right with smooth animation effect; until the new slide is finally placed at the center.

"QxtFlowView in action."

This is a derived work of {http://pictureflow.googlecode.com}{PictureFlow}
