\subsection{Detailed Description}
A thread collecting samples at regular intervals. 

Contiounous signals are converted into a discrete signal by collecting samples at regular intervals. A discrete signal can be displayed by a \hyperlink{class_qwt_plot_series_item}{Qwt\-Plot\-Series\-Item} on a \hyperlink{class_qwt_plot}{Qwt\-Plot} widget.

\hyperlink{class_qwt_sampling_thread}{Qwt\-Sampling\-Thread} starts a thread calling perodically \hyperlink{class_qwt_sampling_thread_a67c4a524736808dc1ba3b81670c0cbd5}{sample()}, to collect and store ( or emit ) a single sample.
