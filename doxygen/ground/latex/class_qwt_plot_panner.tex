\subsection{Detailed Description}
\hyperlink{class_qwt_plot_panner}{Qwt\-Plot\-Panner} provides panning of a plot canvas. 

\hyperlink{class_qwt_plot_panner}{Qwt\-Plot\-Panner} is a panner for a \hyperlink{class_qwt_plot_canvas}{Qwt\-Plot\-Canvas}, that adjusts the scales of the axes after dropping the canvas on its new position.

Together with \hyperlink{class_qwt_plot_zoomer}{Qwt\-Plot\-Zoomer} and \hyperlink{class_qwt_plot_magnifier}{Qwt\-Plot\-Magnifier} powerful ways of navigating on a \hyperlink{class_qwt_plot}{Qwt\-Plot} widget can be implemented easily.

\begin{DoxyNote}{Note}
The axes are not updated, while dragging the canvas 
\end{DoxyNote}
