\subsection{Detailed Description}
The \hyperlink{class_qxt_group_box}{Qxt\-Group\-Box} widget is a collapsive and checkable Q\-Group\-Box. 

Qxt\-Gui \hyperlink{class_qxt_group_box}{Qxt\-Group\-Box} is a checkable group box automatically expanding/collapsing its content according to the check state. \hyperlink{class_qxt_group_box}{Qxt\-Group\-Box} shows its children when checked and hides its children when unchecked.
