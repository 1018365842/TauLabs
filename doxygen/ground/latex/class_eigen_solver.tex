Let D be the block diagonal matrix with the real eigenvalues in 1x1 blocks, and any complex values u+iv in 2x2 blocks \mbox{[}u v ; -\/v u\mbox{]}. Then, the matrices D and V satisfy A$\ast$\-V = V$\ast$\-D.

More precisely, if the diagonal matrix of the eigen values is\-:\par
 $ \left[ \begin{array}{cccccc} u+iv & & & & & \\ & u-iv & & & & \\ & & a+ib & & & \\ & & & a-ib & & \\ & & & & x & \\ & & & & & y \\ \end{array} \right] $ \par
 then, we have\-:\par
 $ D =\left[ \begin{array}{cccccc} u & v & & & & \\ -v & u & & & & \\ & & a & b & & \\ & & -b & a & & \\ & & & & x & \\ & & & & & y \\ \end{array} \right] $
