Enables or disables the specified R\-C\-C interrupts. 

\begin{DoxyNote}{Note}
The C\-S\-S interrupt doesn't have an enable bit; once the C\-S\-S is enabled and if the H\-S\-E clock fails, the C\-S\-S interrupt occurs and an N\-M\-I is automatically generated. The N\-M\-I will be executed indefinitely, and since N\-M\-I has higher priority than any other I\-R\-Q (and main program) the application will be stacked in the N\-M\-I I\-S\-R unless the C\-S\-S interrupt pending bit is cleared. 
\end{DoxyNote}
